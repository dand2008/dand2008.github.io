Hi! My name is Dvir Hasson!

I am a designer, programmer, and photographer from Israel.


I first started building and writing website's code when I completed a children's course during the summer vacation at the Technion.

Initially, programming and building websites was akin to a side hobby, but before long, it evolved into something much greater!

So why write a website?

As everybody already know I could have easily just asked chat GPT or any other AI module to create a website. So why did I choose to write all the code by myself?
Why to go through all the trouble?

The world of coding, programming, and design is my passion and playground.
I love the unparalleled sense of accomplishment that washes over you when you finally crack the code that's been a thorn in your side for an entire week.
I always love to improve the design or the application and stretch the limits. Not stretching the limits to the maximum, but beyond the maximum.
proving that there is no “maximum”, demonstrating that limits are non-existent, solutions are ever attainable, improvements are endless, and the realm of possibilities knows no bounds.

Engaging in Krav Maga is among my hobbies. My journey into learning its techniques and self-defence principles started when I was 5 years old. After a dedicated training of 7 years, I decided to step back and take a brake. The primary catalyst behind this choice...

The primary function of my Mavic Mini revolves around capturing cinematic photos and videos. Lately, an exciting development emerged as I received an invitation to employ my drone for recording and photographing a segment of a local school's graduation party.
